Globalization

What is Globalization? Is Globalization a new phenomenon or not?
Discuss about that!

The term “globalization” as used by social scientists and in particular
discourse has many meanings. We challenge that it is important to
differentiate between globalization as a current political ideology and
what we call structural globalization – the increasing worldwide density
of large – scale interaction networks relative to the density of smaller networks.
We study globalization over the past until now: the trajectory of
globalization in the world (Chase et. al, 2000, 77).

What which is interesting with this exercise is that we can
get insight in the term of globalization as processes of interconnectedness
of social, political and economical processes which have covered all aspects
of humans developments from the past time to the present. Therefore the following
questions circle around in this exercise: what is globalization? When did globalization begin?
Is globalization a new phenomenon, a long – term upward trend, or a cyclical process?
The description of exercise begins partly with presentation and definition of Globalization
and its closest relatives. Then I provide evidence about the age of globalization from
different scholars’ assertions which tell us when the globalization began.
Afterwards I argue about whether globalization is a new phenomenon or not.
In conclusion I pull threads out and I summarize that
how the main questions about globalization are answered.Continue
